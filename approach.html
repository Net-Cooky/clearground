The approach

The work

Support isn't always about fixing something broken

Often, it's about clarifying direction, regaining perspective, and reconnecting with what matters. Less about analyzing every detail of the past. More about helping you make sense of where you are now and how you want to move forward.

Together we explore

What is currently shaping your thinking. What may be keeping you stuck. What matters most moving forward. What is available for you to gain insight in a practical way.

Counselling, at its best, helps simplify

It allows space to slow down, think clearly, and reconnect with your own judgement. Because ultimately, nobody knows your life, values, or goals better than you.

Who this is for

People coping on the surface but feeling stretched underneath. Managing work, family, relationships, or responsibility while privately sensing something isn't quite right.

Feeling stuck, flat, or directionless. A constantly busy or overactive mind. Frustration that life looks fine, but doesn't feel right.

Grief, loss, or significant life changes. Complex or intrusive thoughts. Rumination and being stuck in repeating thought patterns.

Those who value responsibility and personal agency. Want clarity, perspective, and momentum. See personal development as an investment rather than a crisis response.

My work often connects with those in trades, operations, leadership, business ownership, or high-responsibility roles. However, anyone navigating pressure, responsibility, or the quiet weight of keeping things together is welcome.

Important to know

  • This is a privately paid service, not Medicare-rebated
  • I'm not a psychologist, psychiatrist, or medical doctor
  • Not a substitute for urgent or crisis care
  • Intentionally framed as an investment in yourself

What I do offer is time, presence, and practical conversation focused on clarity, agency, and forward movement. The first appointment is offered at a lower entry cost, giving you space to meet, ask questions, and decide whether this approach feels right.
