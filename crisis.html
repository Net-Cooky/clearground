Crisis & Support

Victoria, Australia

If you or someone you care about is in immediate danger, please prioritise safety first.

Call emergency services:

Triple Zero (000)

If there is an immediate risk of harm to you or someone else, call 000 without delay.

What is a crisis?

A crisis can look like:

  • Feeling overwhelmed by thoughts or emotions
  • Suicidal thoughts or intent
  • Severe anxiety, panic, or emotional distress
  • Behaviour that feels unsafe for you or others

You do not need a diagnosis or explanation. If it feels like a crisis, please reach out to one of the services below for urgent support.

24/7 Phone & Online Support

Lifeline (Australia-wide)

13 11 14

Free, 24-hour crisis support and suicide prevention. Text Lifeline on 0477 13 11 14. Online chat is also available via the Lifeline website.

Victoria-specific support

SuicideLine Victoria

1300 651 251

Free phone and online counselling for people at risk, those concerned about someone else, or anyone affected by suicide. Available 24 hours.
